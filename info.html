About Us

At Dougberman Bakery, we take pride in crafting exquisite desserts that elevate your taste buds and create memorable experiences. Our journey began with a passion for baking and a commitment to delivering exceptional flavors. Over the years, our catering services have gained popularity through word of mouth, and we are thrilled to expand our brand to provide an even more amazing experience.

Our vision is to open a full-service physical store on Elgin Street in Ottawa, where we can showcase our delectable and elegant creations. From classic cakes to indulgent pastries, our team of skilled bakers combines traditional techniques with innovative ideas to bring you the finest desserts.

As a bakery that cares about your well-being, we also offer a range of healthier options. We understand the importance of catering to dietary needs, and our menu includes gluten-free, vegan, and low-sugar treats that never compromise on taste.

Thank you for visiting our website. We invite you to explore our menu, immerse yourself in the world of Dougberman Bakery, and allow us to delight you with our passion for baking.
